Crystal Collector

How to Play

  • At the start of the game, you will be presented with a random number of crystals to collect.
  • You must match this random number by adding up the values of the crystals in your collection.
  • Each crystal you have in your collection will have a mystery value assigned at the start of each game.
  • You must find out the value of each of your crystals on your own by clicking on them.
  • Match the number of crystals to collect to your crystal count and you win! Go over and you lose...
